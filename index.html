About Us

Hi, I'm Jonathan Reid, owner and curator of Antique Bookstore, a tranquil haven for book lovers located in Crows Nest, just a short drive from Sydney's city centre. For over 25 years, I've specialized in the sale of rare, antiquarian, and collectible books to individuals, libraries, and dealers across Australia and around the world.

As a member of both the Australian and New Zealand Association of Antiquarian Booksellers (ANZAAB) and the International League of Antiquarian Booksellers (ILAB), I'm committed to the highest ethical and professional standards. Every book in our collection is carefully researched and described with transparency and care, ensuring authenticity and integrity.

With a rotating inventory of around 20,000 titles—ranging from 18th-century atlases and fine bindings to modern first editions and unique curiosities—Antique Bookstore offers something for every bibliophile. I also publish regular catalogues featuring special finds, new arrivals, and staff picks.

Whether you're a serious collector or simply someone who enjoys the charm of old books, I invite you to visit us in-store or browse our selection online. At Antique Bookstore, literature lives on—beautifully preserved and waiting to be discovered.
